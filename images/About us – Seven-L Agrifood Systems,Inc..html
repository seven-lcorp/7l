COMPANY PROFILE

LEX VICTOR D. EUSEBIO

President / CEO – 2018-PRESENT
SEVEN-L AGRIFOOD SYSTEMS, INC.

Seven-L Agrifood Systems, Inc. is a well-established company quite known as a World-Class Distributor of Poultry and Livestock Equipment and Services satisfying the needs of the integrators and their business partners. Seven-L was developed and organized from the old single proprietorship 7L Agri-Food Systems Enterprise since December 19,2011.

7L Agri-Food Systems Enterprises was established on April 18, 2001 after its founder and proprietor, the late LEONIDO G. EUSEBIO resigned from Manila Machinery and Supply Co., Inc., a Sarmiento-owned company.

During the first year, 7L concentrated on sales by indent to large companies such as Robina Farms, Sustamina Agri-Industrial Corporation, Mirandel Dressing Plant (Vitarich business partner), Tyson Agri-Ventures, Inc., Swift Foods, Inc. Foster Foods, etc.

Through hardwork and perseverance of its owner, 7L grew very fast and was able to build a new warehouse and office on its third year of operation and established Corporation named Seven-L on 2012.

After more than 23 years in the industry, 7L acquired distributorships of several more suppliers like Excellence Supply and Services, Inc. KSP AND KAROON BROTHERS from Thailand, TienPhatDat from Vietnam, Systel gas brooders from France, Waterflame environment controls from Thailand, Newtech District prefab buildings from France, AZA International from Italy and more.

Today, the company is managed by his eldest son Lex Victor G. Eusebio who works with the company and his father assistant ,driver and technician since they started the business.

The company Seven-L Agrifood Systems Inc. is now ISO 9001-2015 certified and they continue to grow and have lots of project Nationwide.
