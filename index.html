SEVEN-L AGRIFOOD SYSTEMS, INC.

Seven-L Agrifood Systems, Inc. is a well-established company quite known as a World-Class Distributor of Poultry and Livestock Equipment and Services satisfying the needs of the integrators and their business partners. Seven-L was developed and organized from the old single proprietorship 7L Agri-Food Systems Enterprise since December 19,2011.

7L Agri-Food Systems Enterprises was established on April 18, 2001 after its founder, the late LEONIDO G. EUSEBIO, resigned from Manila Machinery and Supply Co., Inc.

In its first year, 7L focused on sales to large companies such as Robina Farms, Sustamina Agri-Industrial Corporation, Mirandel Dressing Plant (Vitarich partner), Tyson Agri-Ventures, Inc., Swift Foods, Inc., and Foster Foods.

Through dedication and hard work, 7L expanded rapidly, building a new warehouse and office by its third year, and was formally incorporated as Seven-L in 2012.

Today, the company holds distributorships with suppliers like Excellence Supply and Services, Inc., KSP and Karoon Brothers (Thailand), TienPhatDat (Vietnam), Systel gas brooders (France), Waterflame environment controls (Thailand), Newtech District prefab buildings (France), AZA International (Italy), and more.

Now managed by Lex Victor G. Eusebio alongside the original team, Seven-L Agrifood Systems Inc. is ISO 9001-2015 certified and continues to grow nationwide.

Vision
